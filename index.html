About

Who we are

Tinkas Industries Pvt Ltd, formerly known as Origin ITFS Pvt. Ltd. founded in 1993, has a long history of completing important projects, creating connections, and giving customers access to top- notch support. Origin ITFS has a customer-centric business model that spans over 30 years, and it offers unique capabilities across IT and infrastructure to help customers plan and shape their growth around important factors like productivity, cost- effectiveness, and technology. The company initially set out to sell computers and other equipment, but as it steadily grew, it added the selling of the best computer hardware and services as well. Growing tremendously, it expanded its reach to serve other IT-related industries, including significant government IT projects, designing and operating purpose-built IT facilities, and partnering with industry titans like Lenovo, HP, and Dell.

ORIGIN ITFS continued to grow its market share and clientele with the single goal of offering exceptional service and the encouragement of repeat business from clients. With a desire to support next-generation company requirements, Origin ITFS has evolved into Tinkas Industries Pvt Ltd., adding other verticals to the growing client base.

Video Conferencing Solutions


Tinkas is an authorized partner for sales and support for Poly, a leading provider of world-class video conferencing communication solutions. We offer a wide range of video conferencing solutions to help businesses in Tamil Nadu to connect and collaborate effectively, regardless of their location.

Poly offers a variety of video conferencing solutions to meet the needs of different businesses and organizations. Some of the key solutions that we offer include:

  1. Poly Studio X Series: The Poly Studio X Series is an all-in-one video bar that combines video conferencing, wireless presentation, and whiteboarding capabilities. It is designed to provide a simple and streamlined video conferencing experience for small to medium-sized meeting rooms.
  2. Poly Studio Series: The Poly Studio Series is a USB video bar that offers exceptional audio and video quality for huddle rooms and small meeting spaces. It is easy to set up and use, and it is compatible with most popular video conferencing platforms.
  3. Poly Trio: The Poly Trio is a conference phone that offers high-quality audio and video conferencing capabilities for small to medium-sized meeting rooms. It features a touch screen display and is compatible with most popular video conferencing platforms.
  4. Poly RealPresence Group Series: The Poly RealPresence Group Series is a powerful video conferencing system designed for larger meeting rooms and conference rooms. It offers exceptional audio and video quality, as well as advanced features such as content sharing and recording.
